About Our Club

UMAnime exists as a social platform to bring together people who have any level of interest in anime and other connected aspects of similar culture!

Our student-run club is a welcoming place for all and we strive to provide a service that enhances our members’ lives through running multiple initiatives.

These initiatives stem from hosting social gatherings, screening events, and providing our members a socialization space.

We’re a club that has been around since 1997 with memberships tiers that are free and $5 for extra benifits.

These memberships are valid until October 31st of the following school year.

If you have any questions, you can email us at umanimeclub@gmail.com
Hope to see you soon!
